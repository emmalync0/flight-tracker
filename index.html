United MileagePlus Tips
Award flights: Search united.com with "Book with miles" toggled on. Polaris Business SFO→Europe typically costs 60–80k miles + taxes one-way. Saver awards open ~330 days out — June dates should be open now.
Star Alliance partners: United can book Lufthansa, SWISS, Brussels Airlines, Austrian, LOT, and Turkish business class using MileagePlus miles. Lufthansa via FRA or MUC is often available.
Excursionist Perk: With a round-trip award, you get a free one-way within Europe. You could fly SFO→BRU, then BRU→AMS (free), then AMS→SFO.
Paris → Antwerp: Thalys/Eurostar high-speed train from Paris Gare du Nord to Antwerp-Centraal takes ~1hr 50min. Tickets from €29 on thetrainline.com. Book early for the best fares.
Cash fare tips: United Polaris Business fares fluctuate significantly. Tues/Wed departures are often $500–1000 cheaper than weekend departures. Check "flexible dates" on united.com.
